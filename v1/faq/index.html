Frequently Asked Questions

What is Hack It Up?

Hack It Up level based hacking competition which test your cyber security knowledge and ethical hacking skills. Like - Penetrating testing. Which is divided into two parts - Online Round and Offline Round.

What are online and offline rounds?

The competition is divided into two parts. Online Round (23rd September, Sunday) Time - 23/09/2018 - 5.00 pm to 24/09/2018 23.59pm (36hrs) Can be attempted from anywhere. On-Site Round (27th September and 28th September) Day 1 - Workshop by Sriram - Founder of Primefort and OWASP Pondicherry city leader on basics of Cyber Security. Day 2 - Level based Hackathon based on Cyber Security and Ethical Hacking Time - 9.00 am to 4.00pm Venue - SRM IST, Vadapalani, Chennai.

Is there any pre-requisite knowledge required to participate for the event?

There is no such prerequisites for the competition. Plus we would be providing you resources which will help you crack the online round before you attempt it.

Is there any registration fees for this event?

NO.We at WHHC believe that there should not be any cap on learning and encourage to strive for better implementation of knowledge we seek. Just bring your laptop and you are good to go.

Internet access will be provided?

Yes, You will be provided with internet service by the WHHC.

Will I receive a reminder notification before the challenge begins?

You will receive a reminder e-mail 3 hours before the challenge begins only if you have registered for the challenge.

Can we take up the challenges from mobile platforms?

No. We don't support mobile platform for taking up the challenges. We only suggest our users to participate in the challenges from laptop/desktop.

What will happen in case of there is an issue with my computer or Internet connection?

If there is an issue with your computer or your Internet connection, you will not lose your previously submitted answers. They will be saved on our servers. You can log in again and continue hacking from where you stopped. However, the timer will continue running, which is why it is advised that you ensure that your computer/Internet connection is working properly before you begin the challenge.

Is it possible to change my submission after I have submitted a task?

Yes, you can edit and submit the solution to a question (that you can view and edit) any number of times until you get it correct.

What happens if I indulge in plagiarism?

At WHHC, we take cheating/plagiarism very seriously. For more information about consequences of cheating, read our Guidelines.

Will I receive the results of the challenge? If yes, when?

You can view your ranking on the leaderboard for all challenges in which participation is not confidential. This leaderboard is updated in real-time.

Where can I view the solutions for the problems of this challenge?

Once the challenge is finished you will be emailed all the solutions.

Will there be any reimbursement provided and accomodations to students coming from other cities?

There will be NO reimbursement and accommodations to any participants participating in the event

What do winners take home?

The winner take a Sack-full of things with them like Internships from Startups, Opportunity to mentor , Headphones from Audiorica and lots of Swags and goodies to make a memorable event for you. But don’t lose heart, there is lot in the store even if you don’t make to the TOP -3. So keep Hacking. You might find a Jackpot.
